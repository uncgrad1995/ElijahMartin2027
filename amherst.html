Why Amherst

A Personalized Fit

Elijah's interest is based on a campus visit, the baseball setting and the opportunity to combine rigorous academics with a close collegiate community.

Academic Freedom

The open-curriculum environment aligns with Elijah's desire to explore human performance, biomechanics, health and related scientific interests while completing a rigorous liberal-arts education.

Baseball on Campus

Memorial Field's location within campus stood out during his visit. The baseball program feels integrated into the student experience rather than separated from it.

Community Fit

Elijah liked the campus, the town of Amherst, the roster environment and the combination of an intimate college experience with access to the broader Five College community.

“Amherst stood out to me because it combines the academic environment I am looking for with competitive baseball and a campus where the athletic facilities feel like part of the student experience.”
