About Elijah

Elijah Martin is a standout 2-way player known for his leadership, strong bat, defensive versatility, reliability, and consistent pitching. His on-field results always outshine his scouted metrics. Elijah is a gamer, thriving on the competition of never letting the opponent get the best of him, but tipping his cap to him if he does. He combines athleticism with high baseball IQ and a passion for the game. Elijah is a very strong student who would like to pursue playing baseball at the collegiate level, while also working towards a degree from a great college which will prepare him for life after baseball.
